Antipasto Salad

An Italian classic consisting of soft cheeses, slices meats, marinated & fresh Veggies on a bed of mixed greens. We want to share this famlly recipe with you and we want you to share it with yours, so weve added the portions accordingly.

    Chopped Greens (16oz)

  • Romaine
  • Iceberg
  • Arugula

    • Meats (3oz. each)

  • Salami
  • Capicola
  • Pepperoni
  • Prosciutto

    • Veggies

  • Marinated Mushrooms
  • Marinated Eggplant
  • Marinated Green Beans
  • Black Olives
  • Green Olives
  • Artichokes
  • Roasted Red Peppers
  • Chopped Celery
  • Cherry Tomatoes
  • Red Onions

    • Cheeses (Its cheese! Use as much as you see fit)

  • Fresh Mozzarella Cheese
  • Cubed Provalone

    • Garnish & Dressing

  • Garnish with 2 sliced, hard boiled eggs, 1 pepperonchini per person and ground Black Pepper to taste.

    Regular Italian dressing will do or whatever dressing you prefer...just no Ranch, but we think our homemade Vinagarette is perfect for this salad.

Homemade Vinagarette

  • 8 minced cloves of garlic
  • 1/2 tsp of black pepper
  • 2 tsp of sea salt
  • 1/2 cup of extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 cup of freshly squeezed Lemon Juice

    • Mix all ingredients in a large bowl and wisk vigorously for 1 min or until olive oil and lemon blend into one
